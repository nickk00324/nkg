  • Mission

    Nick Kochornswasdi Gallery (NKG) is committed to providing a platform for underappreciated, critically-minded artists through the gallery’s non-traditional operation while remaining independent of market trends.

    NKG assists and supports its artists through exhibitions and finding dedicated patrons to financially support the artists through sales. NKG considers the artists as its primary clients. With the ultimate goal of increasing the artists’ exposure, expand careers, and attract more prominent galleries that can further assist the artists.

  • Bio

    Nick Kochornswasdi Gallery (NKG) is a newly established for-profit contemporary art gallery, founded by Nick Kochornswasdi in Spring 2019. The gallery is committed to presenting critically minded artists through the gallery’s non-traditional platform and operation.
