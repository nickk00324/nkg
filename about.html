  • Mission

    Nick Kochornswasdi Gallery (NKG) is committed to providing a platform for underappreciated, critically-minded artists through the gallery’s non-traditional operation while always remaining independent of market trends.

    NKG assists and supports its artists through exhibitions and finding dedicated patrons to financially support the artists through sales, and because the gallery will always remain financially independent, NKG’s artists will always be free to create the work that they desire. NKG considers the artists as its primary clients, with the ultimate goal of increasing the artists’ exposure, expand careers, and attract more prominent galleries that can further assist the artists that go beyond the scope of the gallery itself.

  • Bio

    Nick Kochornswasdi Gallery (NKG) is a newly established for-profit contemporary art gallery, founded by Nick Kochornswasdi in Spring 2019, committed to exhibiting critically-minded artists. The gallery is currently based in downtown Los Angeles with a brand-new 166 square foot exhibition space with no plans to expand in the near future.
