diego BARRIENTOS

Diego Barrientos (b. 1987, Guatemala City, Guatemala) received his MFA from the California Institute of the Arts in 2019 and a BA from California State University, Northridge in 2016. Recent exhibitions include Alienation, L-Shape Gallery, CalArts, Valencia, CA (2019); And Then There is California, Santa Monica, CA (2019); Other Places Art Fair, San Pedro, CA (2019); and Incognito, Institute of Contemporary Art, Los Angeles, CA (2018). Forthcoming exhibition includes The Life Cycle of Intimacy, New Wight Gallery, Los Angeles, CA. He currently lives and works in Los Angeles.
