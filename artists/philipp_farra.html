philipp FARRA

Philipp Farra (b. 1991, Schönebeck (Elbe), Germany), studied at Grundschule Käthe Kollwitz, Schönebeck, Germany; Ökumenisches Domgymnasium, Magdeburg, Germany; Academy of Visual Arts Leipzig, Leipzig, Germany; HEAD, Geneva, Switzerland; and Academy of Fine Arts, Vienna, Austria prior to earning his MFA from the California Institute of the Arts. Farra will be a participant for the 2019–2020 Whitney Independent Study Studio Program at The Whitney Museum of American Art, New York. He currently lives and works in Los Angeles, New York and Leipzig.
