Some Examples: "The Golden Peaches of Samarkand"
October 12 - December 14, 2019
Opening Reception 7 - 10 p.m.
7931 Nannestad St, Rosemead, CA 91770

Nick Kochornswasdi Gallery is pleased to present, Some Examples: "The Golden Peaches of Samarkand", an exhibition by Bingyang Liu.

Using the mechanics of photography, Liu will present a series of photographs that reference and question familiar food items and their connotations in both popular cultures and media. Both the series and exhibition are titled after Edward H. Schafer’s book, The Golden Peaches of Samarkand. In the book, Schafer mentions the golden, large as goose eggs peaches that were given to China as formal gifts in the seventh century. Though no one has ever seen the peaches, over time, they’ve become a symbol for the foreign and the exotic. Similar to the golden peaches, the photographs are representations, or rather, re-presentations of food items that are portrayed as exotic.
