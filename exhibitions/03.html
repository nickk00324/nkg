MingYan Helen Gu
Nick Kochornswasdi Gallery Presentation
September 14, 2019
Site 1 // 1206 Maple Ave Ste 534, Los Angeles, CA 90015

Nick Kochornswasdi Gallery Presentation is the inaugural presentation celebrating the opening of the gallery’s new Downtown Los Angeles location. Utilizing the aesthetics of a corporate presentation, the gallery’s director, MingYan Helen Gu, will present a Keynote presentation of its mission to the public.
