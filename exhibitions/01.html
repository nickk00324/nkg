Philipp Farra
Inaugurational Speeches
April 28 - May 26 2019
Site 1 // 24700 McBean Pkwy, Broad 14, Valencia, CA 91355

Nick Kochornswasdi Gallery is pleased to announce its inaugural exhibition, a solo exhibition by the artist Philipp Farra called Inaugurational Speeches, the artist’s first solo exhibition with a gallery. The exhibition opens on April 28, 2019 and runs through May 26, 2019. The opening reception will be on April 28, 2019 at 12 pm. For the opening, the gallery will be located at 24700 McBean Pkwy, studio Broad 14, Valencia, CA, 91355

Nick Kochornswasdi Gallery is a progressive gallery exhibiting artists representing the cutting edge of art-making locally and around the world. We are located on a Chromecast, therefore viewing is done by appointment only, with each opening’s location being announced ahead of time.
